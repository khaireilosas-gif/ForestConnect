The Current Landscape

Malaysia is home to some of the world's oldest rainforests. However, we face significant challenges.

Implications of Deforestation

🌊 Flood Risks

Forests act as natural sponges. Without them, Malaysia faces severe flash floods.

🌡️ Rising Temperatures

The "Urban Heat Island" effect is worsening in deforested areas.

🐅 Habitat Fragmentation

Iconic species like the Malayan Tiger are being pushed into isolation.

📉 Soil Erosion

Exposed soil washes away into rivers, killing river life.

Emergency Contacts

Report wildlife crime, poaching, or human-wildlife conflict in Peninsular Malaysia.

Dept. of Environment (JAS)

Report open burning, river pollution, illegal dumping, or industrial smoke.

1-800-88-2727 Call Hotline

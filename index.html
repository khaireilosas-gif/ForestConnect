The Threat of Deforestation ➜

The Problem

Every year, millions of hectares of forest are lost. This destroys habitats and accelerates climate change.

The Causes

Agriculture expansion, logging, and infrastructure development are the primary drivers of global forest loss.

The Solution

Sustainable forestry and reforestation projects are essential to reverse the damage.

About Our Forest Reserves

Forest reserves (Hutan Simpan) are protected areas designated for conservation, sustainable logging, or wildlife protection. They play a crucial role in maintaining the ecological balance.

🌲 Virgin Jungle Reserves

Strictly protected areas serving as natural arboretums and genetic banks for forestry species.

💧 Water Catchment Forests

Forests located upstream of dams and water intake points, critical for water security.

🏕️ Amenity Forests

Forest areas developed for public recreation, eco-tourism, and research.
